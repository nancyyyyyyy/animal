The Jack Chi is a small cross breed known for being affectionate, alert, cheerful, energetic, friendly, loving, playful, protective, and sweet. It is the cross of the Jack Russell Terrier and Chihuahua. The most common colors for Jack Chis are black, black & brown, black & white, black and tan, brown, brown & white, chocolate, cream, dark brown, fawn, golden, light brown, tricolor, and white. To keep it healthy, it will need to get exercise regularly. The Jack Chi commonly participates in agility.
